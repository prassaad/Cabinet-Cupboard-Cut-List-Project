Doors / fronts

Click bays in the design to combine them, then place one door spanning the picked region (it crosses the shelves you included, and stops at the ones you left out).

"+ Door into cell" fits the single clicked bay. Use "Pick cells for door" to combine several bays across shelves into one door. Select a door to change its leaves, front (inset/outset) or Covers.
